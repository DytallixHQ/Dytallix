Economic Functions

DGT Governs. DRT Rewards.

Governance Token: DGT

  • Name: Dytallix Governance Token (DGT)
  • Supply: 1,000,000,000 (fixed, no inflation)
  • Decimals: 18
  • Voting: 1 DGT = 1 vote
  • Features: Transferable, stakeable, earns DRT when staked

DGT Utilities

  • Governance voting on DAO proposals
  • Validator staking to secure the chain
  • Community treasury allocation oversight
  • Protocol parameter adjustments with guardrails

Allocation breakdown

  • Community Treasury: 40% (unlocked)
  • Staking Rewards: 25% (linear over 4 years)
  • Dev Team: 15% (1-year cliff, 3-year linear)
  • Initial Validators: 10% (6-month cliff, 2-year linear)
  • Ecosystem Fund: 10% (linear over 5 years)

Reward Token: DRT

  • Name: Dytallix Reward Token (DRT)
  • Supply Model: Inflationary (~5% annually)
  • Decimals: 18
  • Distribution: Fully automated
  • Features: Transferable and burnable

Emission schedule

  • 60% Block rewards
  • 25% Staking rewards
  • 10% AI module incentives
  • 5% Bridge operations

Burn rules

  • 100% of transaction fees
  • 50% of AI service fees
  • 75% of bridge fees
  • Penalty burns on governance violations
