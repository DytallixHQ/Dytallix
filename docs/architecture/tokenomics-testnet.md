# Tokenomics (Testnet)

Testnet tokens are issued by the faucet and reset on schedule.

> Last updated: 2025-08-20

- Faucet dispenses 100 DYTX per request, 1 per hour.
- Total supply resets to zero on network reset.
- No staking rewards; blocks are subsidized.
- Economics differ from planned mainnet where tokens hold value.
