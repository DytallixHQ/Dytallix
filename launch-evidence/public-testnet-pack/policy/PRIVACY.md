# Dytallix Privacy Policy

## Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Dytallix collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information in connection with the Dytallix blockchain network, associated services, and applications. This policy applies to all users, validators, developers, and other participants in the Dytallix ecosystem.

## Information We Collect

### Blockchain Data

#### Public Blockchain Information
- **Transaction Data**: All transactions are permanently recorded on the public blockchain
- **Addresses**: Blockchain addresses and their associated transaction history
- **Smart Contract Interactions**: Contract calls and their parameters
- **Governance Participation**: Voting records and proposal submissions
- **Validator Information**: Validator performance metrics and delegation data

#### Technical Metadata
- **Block Data**: Block headers, timestamps, and validator signatures
- **Network Metrics**: Node connectivity, performance statistics
- **Protocol Versions**: Software versions and upgrade participation
- **Consensus Information**: Voting and consensus participation records

### Off-Chain Information

#### Voluntary Information
- **Communication**: Information shared in official forums, Discord, or support channels
- **Survey Responses**: Feedback and survey responses from community members
- **Event Participation**: Registration and participation in community events
- **Beta Testing**: Participation in testing programs and feedback

#### Technical Information
- **IP Addresses**: Network IP addresses for operational purposes
- **Browser Information**: Browser type and version for web applications
- **Device Information**: Basic device and operating system information
- **Usage Patterns**: Application usage and interaction patterns

#### Account Information (Optional Services)
- **Email Addresses**: For notifications and communication (if provided)
- **Usernames**: For forum and community platform participation
- **Profile Information**: Self-provided biographical information
- **Preferences**: User interface and notification preferences
